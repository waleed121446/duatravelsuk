Important Updates for Umrah 2025

Travelers preparing for Umrah this year should be aware of several updates:

  • Children’s Access: Children can now easily accompany parents with regular Umrah visas.

  • Mandatory Insurance: Medical coverage is included with visa applications.

  • New Hotels Open: Several premium hotels have recently opened within a short walking distance to the Haram.

  • Technology Integration: Apps like Nusuk are required for managing Rawdah visits and other services.

  • Travel Timing: Avoiding peak holiday periods can lead to a more peaceful and affordable Umrah.

Tips for Booking Early

Booking early ensures not only peace of mind but better deals and hotel availability. Here’s what we recommend:
